- 31 Years -Our Story

Based in La Jolla, we are a family-owned business dedicated to serving wholesome, all-natural frozen yogurt experiences. Its name comes from a story more than 30 years old, a mix of coincidences and many nights of an Irish family meeting to share happiness and luck —values symbolized in many cultures by the frog.

- Process -How It Works

Using only the finest ingredients, natural frozen yogurt is soft-served into a cup or cone of your choosing, using a custom machine.

No powders. No artificial sweeteners. We believe in the goodness of pure, simple fruits and candies to top the best frozen yogurt desserts.
